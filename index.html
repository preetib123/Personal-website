About

Dr Preeti Bajaj is an electronics engineer, who graduated in 1991, earned a postgraduate degree in 1998, and was awarded a doctorate in Electronics Engineering in 2004. With 32 years of rich experience, she currently holds the position of Vice Chancellor of Lovely Professional University, Phagwara, Punjab. Earlier, she served as Vice Chancellor of Galgotias University at Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh and as the Director of an autonomous institute, G. H. Raisoni College of Engineering (GHRCE), Nagpur, BoG Vocational Board of Maharashtra, officiating VC of GHRU Saikheda, Madhya Pradesh and many others.

At present, she is a representative of Corporate/Institutional Members of the Council of Management of AIMA (term 2022-24), Co-Chair, CII Northern Regional Council (2023-24), FICCI Higher Education Committee, CII Higher Education Committee, New Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Steering Committee of NEP 2020 and others.

She is a Fellow of the Institute of Engineers, a Fellow of IETE, a Senior Member of IEEE, a life member of ISTE and the Founder Chair of Nagpur Subsection. She is an Ex-Member, of the General Council, and the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). She is a very active IEEE volunteer for the last 24+ years; working at the region, Council, section level, subsection and branch levels. She, currently, serves as the Vice Chair of Member Activities at IEEE Region 10 Asia Pacific.

She has been very instrumental in the design and development of Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) systems in the public and commercial sectors such as toll collecting, toll audit, highway traffic management system, and Advanced Traveller Information Systems (AITS) with soft/hybrid computing, intelligent systems, and applications of fuzzy logic. Her major contribution is in designing and developing driver assistance systems like driver fatigue detection system, driver’s action and emotion detection system, driver’s behaviour modeling and applying hybrid fuzzy/genetic/PSO to various ITS.

Under her leadership, GHRCE has been awarded Autonomy, three cycles of NBA accreditation to all UG programmes, NAAC accreditation with an A Grade, reaccreditation with an A+ grade by scoring 3.51, TEQIP 1 Grant of INR 10 Cr and TEQIP 2 grant of INR 6 Cr, ARIIA Ranking - pan India 2nd, and many more. GHRCE achieved great milestones and is still continuing with the broad vision that she has set. A few flagship programs like GHR Connect, GHR Career Connect, Ignite, AI Summit, and Smart City Summit reached out to all corners of India. GHRCE received grants worth INR 27+ Cr. viz: DST-NIDHI-TBI, AICTE-Margdarshan, UGC-Paramarsh, World Bank-TEQIP-I & II. Her passion is to connect industry, institutions and the community in one place and bring need-based innovations through technology for students by students which resulted in the establishment of four COE in Biomedical, AI-ML, Data Science, and Design. She has trained more than 1000 faculty members in different verticals like research, OBE, academic processes, etc. She has chaired many sessions in international conferences in India & abroad. Dr Bajaj is known for bringing reforms to the educational system in Maharashtra and her experiments in autonomy. She has been advocating for drafting technical education domain, project-based learning, education 4.0 in higher and technical education.

She has taught soft computing to undergraduate and postgraduate students of Computer Science and Electronics Engineering. 13 scholars have completed their PhD under her supervision and she is a PhD examiner at many universities. She has authored & co-authored 150+ reviewed publications with 1200+ citations, an H-index ratio of 17 & i10-Index ratio of 23. She has co-authored three book chapters in an international publication and has 8 patents to her credit.

Dr Bajaj holds several awards like the Best Teacher award, the ‘Bhaskar Woman’s Award’ and ‘Faculty of the Month'. She is a recipient of the IEEE MGA Achievement Award 2021 for exemplary contribution and dynamic leadership in achieving a rapid transformation of IEEE educational activities with several geographic units, the India Council Lifetime Achievement Award, a Special Jury Award for FICCI Road Safety Awards, ‘The Phenomenal She’ the award launched by Indian National Bar Association (INBA), Fifth Edition of Coffee Table Book on the occasion of International Women’s Day 2023.

She is the founder, General Chair of ICETET series of 7 IEEE conferences. She has also been recognized as a resource person by the NBA for creating awareness of Outcome Based Education and OBA and also is an Ex-member of the General Council of the NBA. She was invited to deliver talks and present her research work in countries like Singapore, Hong Kong, the UK, Japan, Malaysia, Mauritius, and the USA. Her efforts have turned 208 projects into patents, transforming a private engineering institution into India's premier Engineering and Research Institute. Her efforts took the regional institution to be featured in the Annual report of the Indian Patent Office 6 times, being one of the top 10 patent filers in the country.

During her tenure at Galgotias University, Dr Bajaj could bring a substantial increase in the NIRF Ranking, She was instrumental to get NAAC A+ (3.37), 12 B, SIRO recognition by the Department of Science and Technology (DST), NBA of Pharmacy and management accreditation, reaccreditation of three engineering programs, She also was very effective in the creation of academic processes, SOPs and mandates. Under her able guidance, the university could sign 400 MOUs (national & international), and collaborate with industry. She has set up a Fab lab, incubator, and executed Toycothon and hardware hackathons and mentored more than 100 start-ups on the campus. She has streamlined NCC, NSS, and research processes, set up RAC and streamlined the PhD processes. With her rigorous efforts on branding for 3 years, Galgotias university is known as the fastest-growing university of Central India. Her consistent efforts have taken the university into the top 10 patent files in the country.

Her expertise is in accreditations and rankings, internationalisations, streamlining reforms in academic examinations through interdisciplinary education, activity-based examinations, and outcome-based education. She is very active and guided a number of institutions on accreditations NAAC / NBA. She was on the global committee of accreditations for 2016-2017. She is a tech lady, putting all her energy into nurturing systems and processes in HEI, especially for research, innovations and entrepreneurship that sets the roadmap for ranking and accreditations. She joined LPU on 16 November 2022, taking LPU to the next level.
